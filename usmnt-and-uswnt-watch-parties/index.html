Home of US Soccer Support in Las Vegas

Your home for all AO Vegas watch parties is McMullan's Irish Pub.  We recommend getting there at least 15 minutes before the match starts, unless its a World Cup related match and then you'll likely need to be there a few hours early.  Beer specials are typically on Dale's Pale Ale, but can vary from match to match.

Address: 4650 W Tropicana Ave #110, Las Vegas, NV 89103
